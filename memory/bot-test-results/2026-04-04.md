## 🔴 ALERT: Category L (Legal) scored 35.3 — CRITICAL FAILURE

### Diagnosis

**Root cause: Intent classifier does not recognize "legal/regulatory" queries.**

1. **L01 (NOM-251)**: The bot misclassified a regulatory question as "Reporte de productos que se vendió más" and entered the date-slot-filling flow. The RAG/informational pipeline was completely bypassed. The classifier likely keyed on "alimentos" and matched it to product reports.

2. **L11 (Distintivo H)**: The bot correctly classified as "informacional" but the RAG retrieval found no matching content. The fallback response ("no pude identificar la ruta exacta") is designed for POS navigation questions, not general knowledge. The bot has no knowledge base entries for regulatory/legal topics.

### Recommended Fixes

1. **Add legal/regulatory intent** to the classifier, or expand the "informacional" intent to handle regulatory topics without defaulting to POS navigation fallback.
2. **Expand RAG knowledge base** with basic food safety regulation content (NOM-251, Distintivo H, NOM-093, COFEPRIS basics). These are extremely common questions from restaurant operators.
3. **Improve fallback for informacional** — when RAG finds no match, say "No tengo información sobre ese tema todavía" instead of asking about modules.
4. **L01 classifier fix is highest priority** — misclassifying a text question as a sales report is a fundamental intent error.
