## 🔴 ALERT: Category L (Legal/Normativa) scored 29.0 — well below 70 threshold

### Diagnosis

**Root Cause:** The bot has NO knowledge base or RAG content for restaurant industry regulations (NOM-251, Distintivo H, food safety laws). When asked legal/regulatory questions:

1. **L01 (NOM-251):** Intent classifier misrouted to "Reporte de productos más vendidos" — the word "alimentos" likely triggered a false match with product reports. Bot asked for a date range instead of answering.
2. **L11 (Distintivo H):** Intent correctly tagged "informacional" but the RAG retrieval returned nothing useful, falling back to a generic "tell me what module you're in" response.

### Recommended Fixes

1. **Add regulatory knowledge base:** Create RAG documents covering NOM-251, Distintivo H, NOM-093, and other restaurant-relevant Mexican regulations
2. **Improve intent classifier:** Add a "legal/regulatory" intent category to prevent misrouting to report intents
3. **Better fallback for informacional:** When RAG returns no results, say "No tengo información sobre ese tema" instead of asking about modules
